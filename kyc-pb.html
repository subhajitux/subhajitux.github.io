Introduction

PhonePe Business App is a vital tool, empowering businesses with seamless digital transactions. However, the existing KYC process faced usability and compliance challenges, leading to high user drop-off rates. This case study showcases how a user-centric approach transformed the KYC process, resulting in improved user experience and regulatory compliance.

What is Onboarding/KYC in PhonePe Business

The users of PhonePe Business app are required to complete a mandatory onboarding/KYC for them to become verified. Without undergoing onboarding/KYC, the user cannot be verified to use the primary features of the app, such as receiving payments, ordering smart speakers, etc.

Information Architecture

We designed a user-centric KYC process in the PhonePe Business App by simplifying it into three clear steps and ensuring responsive design. Grouping related data enhances user comprehension, with ongoing iterative testing for optimization.

User Interface Design

We used the PhonePe Business Design System to create the user interface of the KYC flow.

Results

The new KYC flow resulted in a significant reduction in drop-off rates and an increase in user satisfaction. We also saw a decrease in the number of support tickets related to the KYC process.

Learnings

One of the key learnings from this project is the importance of user research. By conducting user interviews early on in the process, we were able to identify the pain points and needs of our users. This helped us to design a solution that was truly user-centered. Finally, we learned the importance of simplicity. The original KYC flow was too long and complex, which was one of the main reasons why users were dropping off. By simplifying the flow and grouping relevant data points together, we were able to make it much easier for users to complete the process.
